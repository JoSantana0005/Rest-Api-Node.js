Categories

The UFC categories are where the fighter will fight depending on their body weight, these categories are the following:

  • - Flyweight
  • - Bantamweight
  • - Feartherweight
  • - Lightweight
  • - Welterweight
  • - Middleweight
  • - Semimediumweight
  • - Heavyweight

Flyweight

The flyweight category is the lightest category in the UFC, the fighters in this category weigh between 56.7 kg and 52.2 kg.

Bantamweight

The bantamweight category is the second lightest category in the UFC, the fighters in this category weigh between 61.2 kg and 56.7 kg.

Feartherweight

The feartherweight category is the third lightest category in the UFC, the fighters in this category weigh between 65.8 kg and 61.2 kg.

Feartherweight Fighter

Jose Aldo
