¿What is about?

The UFC stands for the Ultimate Fighting Championship, and is an organization of enormous size and influence within mixed martial arts, known as MMA. The UFC has the signings of the best international fighters, in various weights, and they organize events in different parts of the world, such as the United States or the United Arab Emirates. Spain could be one of the next destinations for this private organization, and specifically Madrid, with the Santiago Bernabéu Stadium bidding.

UFC categories

The UFC categories are where the fighter will fight depending on their body weight, these categories are the following:

  • - Flyweight
  • - Bantamweight
  • - Feartherweight
  • - Lightweight
  • - Welterweight
  • - Middleweight
  • - Semimediumweight
  • - Heavyweight

UFC Fighters

The fighters are what give life to the UFC, whether they are fighters who have practiced jiu-jitsu, kickboxing, Wrestling, Muay Thai, Boxing, etc... in which two fighters go into the ring to fight to win a title or add more victories to their record, some fighters like Jon Jones, Conor Mcgregor, Shogun Rua, Justin Gaethej, among others.
